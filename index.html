Why PCOS Matters?

Common Symptoms

PCOS causes irregular periods, weight gain, acne, and hormonal imbalances, affecting millions of women worldwide.

Long-Term Health Risks

It increases the risk of type 2 diabetes, heart disease, and infertility, making early detection crucial.

Using Data Science

Machine learning models help predict PCOS risk factors, enabling early intervention and better treatment plans.

What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder affecting millions of women globally. It is characterized by irregular menstrual cycles, high androgen levels, and ovarian cysts. The condition impacts **reproductive health, metabolism, and overall well-being**.

Our project aims to use **data-driven approaches** to analyze PCOS trends, detect risk factors, and provide insights into effective lifestyle modifications.
